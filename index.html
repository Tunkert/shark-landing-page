Shark Attacks

Per year there are:

  • 333 Great White Attacks
    • 52 of which are fatal
  • 131 Tiger Shark Attacks
    • 34 of which are fatal
  • 177 Bull Shark Attacks
    • 25 of which are fatal

Did you know?

We may actually be harming ourselves by fishing. Sharks are important for health and well being of us. If sharks die off our ocean, which we depend upon for oxygen, will die off.

Whitetip Sharks

Whitetip sharks are some of the most dangerous types of sharks, attacking humans who are in ship wrecks and plane crashes into the ocean.
